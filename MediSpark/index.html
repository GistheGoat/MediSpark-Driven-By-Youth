MediSpark: By the Youth

The Idea:

MediSpark is a medical nonprofit led by the youth to enhance and strengthen our understanding of the medical field and develop real-world skills.

The Goal:

Our goal is to help the world by supporting communities with challenges in the medical field. MediSpark plans to host fundraisers, raffles, trivia, contests related to healthcare, and partner with a health provider to fundraise and raise awareness.

Who We Are Helping:

We aim to help the general public by collaborating with a health provider. Through this partnership, we intend to fundraise and provide free health kits, medical bags, and pop-up health screenings to communities in need.

What Makes Us Different:

MediSpark is a youth-led nonprofit, setting us apart from other organizations in the healthcare space.

Contact Us:
